## The Viral Engine

### Share cards are the ENTIRE growth strategy

Every game ends with a copy-pasteable score card:
```
Worduel Daily #1 — 4/6
⬛⬛🟨⬛🟩
🟩⬛⬛🟨🟩
🟩🟩⬛🟩🟩
🟩🟩🟩🟩🟩
🔥 3 | npx worduel
```

Every share = an install command in the wild. This is how Wordle grew from 90 to 300,000 players in 2 months.

### Three viral hooks

1. **The share card** — visual, intriguing, includes install command
2. **The jailbreak challenge** — "try to get Claude to reveal the answer (you can't)"
3. **The witty AI host** — screenshot-worthy Claude responses people want to share

## The One Rule

> Every design decision should answer: "Does this make people more likely to share their score?"

The emoji grid is a solved viral mechanic. Your job is to make the path from "finish game" to "paste score on Twitter" as frictionless as possible. One copy, one paste, done.

The game markets itself. Everything else just accelerates the flywheel.
